Haley.

Haley Halverson was born in 1993 in Fremont, Nebraska. She is the eldest child of Chuck and Colleen Halverson and a big sister to Jonas and Elijah. Haley was homeschooled from K-12 and began taking college courses in her sophomore year of high school. During high school she spent time reading, writing, singing in worship band, and participating in several theater productions. After high school graduation, she attended Hillsdale College in Hillsdale, Michigan on a Grewcock Scholarship. She will graduate in spring of 2015 with a double major in Christian Studies and Politics. During her college career she has been on the Dean’s List, did coursework at Oxford in England, an Internship at Liberty Fund as well as honored as a student in the WHIP Program (Washington Hillsdale Internship Program) and a member of the Kappa Kappa Gamma Sorority. While in Washington DC. Haley completed internships at The Heritage Foundation and at Family Research Council. She is a gifted writer and a woman of strong faith in Christ. Those who know her consider themselves blessed!
